VerboseVR

StudyUP!

StudyUp was a project started to reduce inefficincies on student workflows. By analyzing critical information points in TensorFlow and Javascript, we created a tool that minimizes study time, and maximizes learning. To do this, the machine is first modeled after typical textbooks, and finds ways to understand vocab weight within usage against a file or within the textbook. By highlighting these elements the student is quickly able to reference and understand the most impactful parts that they can now easily look over.
